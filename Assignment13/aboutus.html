About Us

Welcome to The Cheeky Chef — your new favorite silly dining spot downtown!

Our mission is simple, serve delicious food with a mischievous twist. We believe dining should be fun, surprising, and unforgettable. Every dish on our menu is created with bold flavors, fresh ingredients, and a whole lot of personality. Come meet our lovely hosts and servers who make you feel at home, watch our talented bartenders whip up beautiful and tasty drinks, and send your thanks to our hardworking, food loving chefs!

Founded in 2025 by a group of silly food lovers, The Cheeky Chef isn't just about eating — it's about experiencing food in a whole new way. Whether you're craving something classic with a spicy or sweet twist, or looking for a new flavor adventure, we've got you covered!

Come hungry. Leave cheeky!
