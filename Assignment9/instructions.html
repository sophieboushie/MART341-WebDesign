Fav Cookie Recipe

I've used this cookie recipe for the past few years and everyone has loved it. Even my friends who don't enjoy sweets wanted to take a few cookies home with them. Here I'll list the ingredients, supplies and instructions needed for the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Salted Butter
  • 1 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 cup Light Brown Sugar
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Eggs
  • 3 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 Cups Chocolate Chips

Supplies

  • Mixing Bowl
  • Measuring Cups and Spoons
  • Mixing Spoon and Ice Cream Scoop
  • Baking Sheet

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt in bowl
  3. Cream butter and both sugars together
  4. Beat in 2 eggs and vanilla
  5. Mix in dry ingredients until combined
  6. Mix in chocolate chips
  7. Ice cream scoop and shape cookies onto baking sheet
  8. Bake for 8-10 minutes
