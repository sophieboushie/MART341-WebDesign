About Me

This cookie recipe has been one of the only recipe's that have stuck in my rotation for years.

I first tried it out on my own when I lived at home and it was the exact kind of chocolate chip cookie I was looking for!

Halloween 2022 was when I doubled the recipe to bring to my friends Halloween party. I was so happy to see everyone rave about the cookies and even ask to keep some or take some home.
